Hello!
Welcome to the single cell map!
You can open the dataset by clicking on the button to your left.
After you open the dataset map you can either see the different cell annotations or specific genes by clicking on the buttons on the left side.
nCount= number of UMIs
nFeature= number of genes
group= the experimental group the cells come from
percent.mito= percentage of mitochondrial genes detected

You can also click on the cluster number to see the marker genes for each cluster.
avg logFC= average log fold change
p val adj= adjusted p value; note this may be higher than 0.05 as genes with nominal p value less than 0.05 were kept
pct.1=percentage of cells expressing the gene in the cluster
pct.2=percentage of cells expressing the genes in all other clusters
p-value=nominal p value

Under the "genes" tab, the top 100 upregulated genes from NAC (based on previous bulk RNA seq) are shown, but you can search for any gene and see its expression level on the cellular map.

Finally under the genes tab, you can manually select a subgroup of cells by drawing a square (after clicking on the "square" button on the top left corner) and a violin plot with the gene expression for the selected cells pops up on the right side.
